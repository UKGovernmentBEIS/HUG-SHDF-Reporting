Import new lodgements from TrustMark

How it works

Lodgements that have been made in TrustMark over the past month by your installers will be added to this platform.

The platform uses TrustMark's Application Programming Interface (API) to gather the lodgement data efficiently and securely.

What happens next

New lodgements will be listed on the relevant property in your register.

You will then need to update the properties' statuses to 'Complete' if there are no further measures to be installed at the property.
